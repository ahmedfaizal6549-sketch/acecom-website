Our Story

Since 2015, Acecom Lanka has been Sri Lanka's premier destination for premium laptops and technology products. Located at the heart of Colombo's Majestic City, we have built a reputation as the most trusted and authorized retailer of top laptop brands in the country.

Our journey is rooted in a simple mission: to make quality technology accessible to everyone while providing unparalleled customer service. From individual customers to corporate clients, we've served over 5,000 happy customers who trust us for their technology needs.
